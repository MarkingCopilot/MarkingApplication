Problem 3: Find the limit: lim(x→2) (x² - 4)/(x - 2)

Solution:

lim(x→2) (x² - 4)/(x - 2)

Factor: (x² - 4) = (x - 2)(x + 2)

= lim(x→2) (x - 2)(x + 2)/(x - 2)

= lim(x→2) (x + 2)

= 4
