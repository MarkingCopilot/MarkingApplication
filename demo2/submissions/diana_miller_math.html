Problem 1: Find the derivative of f(x) = 3x² + 2x - 5

Solution:

Problem 2: Evaluate ∫(4x³ - 2x + 1)dx

∫(4x³ - 2x + 1)dx

Problem 3: Find the limit: lim(x→2) (x² - 4)/(x - 2)

Solution:

Direct substitution gives 0/0 (indeterminate form)

Factor numerator: x² - 4 = (x + 2)(x - 2)

lim(x→2) (x + 2)(x - 2)/(x - 2)

= lim(x→2) (x + 2)

= 2 + 2 = 4
